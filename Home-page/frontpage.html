About Us

At Meat Grid, we're transforming how the meat industry operates in Bangladesh by bringing together technology, transparency, and data-driven insight. Our platform is designed to connect every key stakeholder in the meat supply chain — farmers, wholesalers, retailers, slaughterhouses, researchers, health authorities, and policymakers — under a single, intelligent system.
